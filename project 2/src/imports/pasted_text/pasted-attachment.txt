I would like you to create a full, professional, and well-structured school management website that will handle all academic and administrative activities in a clear, simple, and reliable way. The system should be designed to support different roles in a school environment, including the Admin, Headmaster, Teachers, Class Teachers, and Students, where each user receives the right tools and performs specific tasks without confusion.

The main target of this website is to build a complete academic workflow system where everything starts from proper setup and flows step by step until reports are generated and viewed. The system should not be complicated but must work correctly, especially in important areas like marks, messaging, and reporting.

The homepage should act as the central gateway of the system. It must have a clean and modern design with clear navigation to all main sections such as dashboard, user management, classes, marks, reports, messaging, and academic terms. Instead of placing messages in the center, a small popup should appear at the corner of the screen to welcome users and guide them. The homepage should also briefly show each role so that users understand what they will access once they log in.

The Admin is the foundation of the entire system. The Admin receives full access and control over all features and is responsible for building the structure of the school inside the system. The Admin creates the Headmaster, Teachers, Class Teachers, and Students. Before adding students, the Admin must first create teachers and classes because students depend on these. The Admin assigns teachers to subjects and classes, selects a class teacher for each class, and creates academic terms and years separately. The Admin also monitors whether marks and attendance are being recorded correctly and ensures the system runs without errors.

The Headmaster receives a dashboard focused on supervision and school performance. The Headmaster can view all reports, monitor how students and classes are performing, and ensure teachers are doing their work correctly. The Headmaster does not change marks but reviews them. The Headmaster can also send announcements and communicate with teachers and students. However, communication between the Headmaster and teachers remains private and is not visible to students.

Teachers receive a subject-based workspace that only shows the classes and students they are assigned to. This helps them focus only on their work. Teachers are responsible for adding marks and recording attendance, and this feature must work properly without saving errors. Teachers can also communicate through the messaging system with students, other teachers, and the Headmaster. Their role is limited to academic input, and they do not manage system structure.

The Class Teacher has a wider responsibility compared to subject teachers. The Class Teacher receives access to all students in a specific class and can see all their academic information across subjects. The Class Teacher manages student records, adds conduct and remarks, and generates reports. These reports include term reports and yearly reports, based on the academic periods created by the Admin. The Class Teacher plays a key role in organizing all academic data into meaningful results.

Students receive a simple dashboard where they can view their personal academic information. They can see marks, attendance, conduct, and reports generated for each term and year. Students cannot edit any data, which keeps the system secure. They can also communicate with teachers, classmates, and the Headmaster using the messaging system.

The messaging system is an important feature and must work as a real conversation system, not charts or diagrams. It allows communication between student and student, student and teacher, teacher and teacher, teacher and Headmaster, and class group discussions. Some conversations, like between teachers and the Headmaster, must remain private and not visible to students. Messages must send and appear correctly without delays or errors.

The reporting system must be clear and accurate. Reports are generated by the Class Teacher using marks, attendance, and conduct. These reports are organized by term and by academic year. Students can view their reports, and the Headmaster can review all reports across the school. This ensures proper tracking of academic progress.

In addition to these features, the system should include light and dark mode for better user experience, responsive design for all devices, and a clean modern interface. The design should focus on clarity and usability so that every user can easily understand what they receive and what they are expected to do.

In conclusion, this website should function as a complete and reliable academic system where the Admin sets up the structure, Teachers provide academic data, Class Teachers organize and generate reports, the Headmaster supervises performance, and Students follow their progress. The system must work smoothly, avoid errors, and focus on real school needs while maintaining a professional and modern design.
